Privacy Policy

At SciAstra, we value your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

We collect information such as your name, email address, and payment details when you use our services or sign up for our newsletter.

Your personal information is used to process payments, provide customer support, and send updates about our courses and blog posts.

3. Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data. We do not share your information with third parties without your consent unless required by law.

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our website. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

5. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.
